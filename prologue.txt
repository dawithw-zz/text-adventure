You are playing as Agent 47, a formerly retired professional assassin. Agent 47 has left behind his life as an assassin and retreated to a church in Sicily to seek peace. He works as a gardener for Father Vittorio, his best friend and mentor. But, when Vittorio is kidnapped, he decides to go back to his old job as an assassin and hunt down the kidnappers. He contacts his agency, who thought he was dead, and makes a deal to work for them if they can help him locate Vittorio. The agency provides the location of the kidnappers residence where Vittorio is assumed to be held captive.
